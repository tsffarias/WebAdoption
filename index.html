Welcome to WebAdoption

WebAdoption is a non-profit organization located in Brazil, Ilha Solteira-SP, where we provide short and long term humane solutions to the homeless dogs and cats population, exposed to abuses, hunger and often, a cruel death..
We transform rescued dogs and cats (sometimes very scared or shy) into safe pets for the right people-companions.
With us, a pet stops subsisting and starts living! Make a donation today and help us save more lifes :)
